Individual Unity Projects

Android VR shooter with Bluetooth controller.

BSc Thesis

An interactive and immersive Virtual Reality shooter game for Android phones through the Unity3D engine. The player could navigate through the terrain, shoot or throw hand grenades at enemies by using a bluetooth hand controller. The enemies could detect when the player was aiming at them and hide behind trees. Enemies tried to walk away from grenades if they were close to them, otherwise they would try to deal as much damage to the player as possible.

Integrating AR with BIM in Construction

MSc Thesis

The project included superimposing BIM 3D objects to real-life infrastructure using ARCore. The 3D models were downloaded at runtime using AWS services in order to stay up-to-date with the designing team. The application allowed visualising building designs in the real world to allow scrutinization, device localisation (with respect to BIM model) in the physical space using printed images, and instruction animations in the physical space by using AR Core's Augmented Images.
